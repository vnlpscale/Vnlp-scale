## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-18

### Added

- Version 2 chunk-addressable store format.
- Atomic manifests, exclusive writer lock, and crash-tail recovery.
- SHA-256 checksums for every payload blob.
- Recursive low-rank and quantized residual codec stages.
- Chunk-level safetensors ingestion and resume.
- Bounded-memory NumPy Llama reference runtime.
- Experimental bounded-memory PyTorch runtime.
- Hardware feasibility planner and 1T reference scenarios.
- Automated codec, store, corruption, ingestion, inference, and planner tests.
